FAQs.

Everything you need to know about permanent lighting.

Can I use the lights year round?

Absolutely! Customers use them for birthdays, family gatherings, game nights, parties, and everyday accent lighting. With 16 million color options, you can make any event special.

Can I do warm/soft white?

Yes! You can create almost any color using the color wheel, including matching the warmth of your existing exterior lighting. Perfect for everyday elegance.

How long do the lights last?

Everlights are rated for 50,000 hours. That's over 20 years if you run them 3 to 5 hours nightly. These aren't your typical holiday lights that burn out after one season.

Are the lights visible during the day?

Barely! The tracks are custom color matched to your trim, and the low profile lenses make them virtually invisible during daylight. Your home looks clean and elegant year round.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installs are completed in 1 day or less, depending on the size of your home. We handle everything. You just enjoy the results.

What areas do you serve?

FAQs.

Everything you need to know about permanent lighting.

Product & Installation

How long do the lights last?

Residential systems are rated for 50,000 hours—over 20 years of nightly use. Commercial systems last even longer at 100,000 hours. These aren't your typical holiday lights that burn out after one season.

Are the lights visible during the day?

Barely! The EverLights tracks are custom color-matched to your trim, and the low-profile "cloud" lenses make them virtually invisible during daylight hours. Your home looks clean and elegant year-round.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 1-2 days depending on the size of your home. Commercial projects vary based on building size. We'll give you an exact timeline during your free consultation.

Will you damage my roof or gutters?

No. Our trained professionals use specialized mounting techniques that protect your home. We never drill into shingles or compromise your roof's integrity.

Can I install these myself?

While EverLights can be purchased for DIY installation, we strongly recommend professional installation to ensure proper color matching, clean wire management, and warranty coverage. Plus, you'll never have to climb on your roof!

Smart Features & Control

Is it smart-home compatible?

Yes! EverLights integrates with Amazon Alexa and Google Home. Control your lights with voice commands or through the smartphone app.

How many colors can I choose from?

The system offers 16 million color combinations with up to 250 colors per sequence. You can create custom patterns, use holiday presets, or design your own unique displays.

Can I schedule the lights to turn on automatically?

Absolutely. The smartphone app includes an integrated calendar where you can set daily schedules, timers, and customize which days your lights display. Set it once and forget it!

Do the lights work during power outages?

The lights require electricity to operate, so they won't function during a power outage. However, your programmed schedules and patterns are saved and will resume automatically when power is restored.

Warranty & Maintenance

What is the warranty?

EverLights come with a 5-year product warranty covering all LED bulbs, tracks, and components. Let It Glow provides a 2-year labor guarantee on our installation workmanship.

What if a bulb burns out?

Individual LED bulbs are replaceable and covered under warranty. If you experience any issues, just give us a call and we'll take care of it.

Do the lights require maintenance?

Minimal maintenance required! An occasional wipe-down to remove dust or debris is all you need. No more wrestling with tangled cords or replacing burnt-out bulbs every year.
